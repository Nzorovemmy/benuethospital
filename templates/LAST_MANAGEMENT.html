Welcome to the Electronic Patient Management System (EPMS)

Use the navigation menu to access the various patient management services:

  • Register Patient: Add new patients and store their basic information.
  • Medical Records Management: Access and update patient medical records.
  • Appointment Scheduling: Schedule, reschedule, or cancel patient appointments.
  • Billing and Insurance Processing: Manage patient billing, payments, and insurance claims.
  • Treatment and Care Tracking: Track patient treatments, care plans, and follow-ups.
